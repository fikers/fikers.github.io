Meet Our Experienced Team

Dr. Syed Azam

Dr. Azam is Board certified in Internal Medicine, he has been practicing in the Coachella valley since 1999. He has special interest in treating Diabetes Mellitus, Coronary artery diseases, Hypertension, Hyperlipidemia, Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease (COPD), Osteoarthritis, Asthma, and Preventive Medicine.

"I appreciate all my patients for the trust they have in our practice. In our office we will do the best to provide the care they need."

Dr. Soujanya Marupuru

Dr. Marupuru is a board certified family medicine doctor who did residency at Salina Family Health Center, Kansas University. Dr Marupuru has been practicing in the Coachella Valley since 2009 and also has experience in urgent care medicine, working in an urgent care clinic in town before she joined Indus Medical Associates.

"I am passionate about preventive medicine. I believe in educating my patients so that they can take ownership of their health. Together we will do the best to optimize your health, so that you can have a good quality of living."

NP Jeffrey Hong

NP Hong is a nationally board-certified Adult/Gerontology Primary Care Nurse Practitioner in the Coachella Valley since 2017.  He completed his training at UCLA in 2015. He believes in helping people achieve better health through a healthier lifestyle.

"I want each one of my patients to feel strong and healthy. This means I won't just treat your illness. I'll do my best to help you avoid future health problems."

NP Sin Wee Koh-Gale

NP Sin Wee has been a Registered Nurse over ten years, and he graduated from UCLA in 2014 as a Nurse Practitioner specialized in Adult-Gerontology Primary Care.

"I believe that compassionate care comes before cure at every patient contact. In closed collaboration with Dr. Azam, he offers patient education and effective treatment to optimize health and well being."

NP Catherine Warne

NP Cathy has been a family Nurse Practitioner since 2002. She has worked in the Coachella valley since 2004 and at Indus Medical Associates since 2015. Cathy’s focus is health prevention and disease prevention through a healthy lifestyle.

"I am excited to help patients be the best they can be with diet, exercise and other lifestyle modifications."
